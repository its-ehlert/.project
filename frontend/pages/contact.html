Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section, or by using the tracking number sent to your email.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Books must be in their original condition. Please visit our Returns page for detailed information.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Check our Shipping page for details.

How can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting our customer service team. After that, the order will be processed for shipping.
